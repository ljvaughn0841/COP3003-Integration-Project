ZoneTimer::calcTimeEarned.

Overrides TimerMode's pure virtual calcTimeEarned if the conditions are met the user recieves double their bet with half the difficulty reduction. This is essentially difficulty tax evasion that incentivises time comitments in order to reduce your difficulty by about a level.
